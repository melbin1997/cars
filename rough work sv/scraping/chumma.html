Latest on Swift Dzire

January 7, 2016 :One of the highest selling sedans in the country, the Maruti Suzuki Swift DZire, has recently been fitted with its manufacturer’s new Auto Gear Shift (AGS) technology, the company announced on Thursday. According to a press release, the DZire has now become the first diesel car - and the fourth model in Maruti Suzuki’s portfolio in India - to come with the AGS technology, also known as Automated Manual Transmission (AMT).
